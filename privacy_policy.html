Privacy Policy

Note: This is an official copy of the Privacy Policy of Urban Administration & Development Department (UADD). The original version is available at https://enagarpalika.mp.gov.in/privacypolicy (accessible only within India).

Urban Administration & Development Department (UADD) does not collect personal information about individuals except when such individuals specifically provide such information on a voluntary basis, such as personal information for subscription/registration.

Personal information on individual users will not be sold or otherwise transferred to unaffiliated third parties without the approval of the user at the time of collection. At points of data collection, the user will have the opportunity to indicate whether he or she would like to "opt out" of receiving promotional, informational, or service-related offerings from UADD and/or third parties.

UADD reserves the right to perform statistical analyses of user behavior and to use such analyses to measure interest in and characteristics of various areas of the site, and to inform advertisers of such information. UADD will provide only aggregate data from these analyses to third parties. Also, users should be aware that UADD may sometimes permit third parties to offer subscription and/or registration-based services through UADD's site. UADD is not responsible for, and users should check, the privacy policies of such third parties before providing personally identifiable information.

Log Data

The UADD may gather certain information about a user, including but not limited to Internet Protocol (IP) addresses, domain names, operating system, date and/or time of visit, and pages visited. The UADD makes no active attempt to link such information with the identity of individuals visiting this mobile application unless required by law, or for the purpose of investigating, preventing, managing, recording, or responding to unlawful, unauthorized, fraudulent, or other unethical conduct, or any law enforcement purposes.

Service Providers

We may employ third-party companies and individuals for the following reasons:

  • To facilitate our Service.
  • To provide the Service on our behalf.
  • To perform Service-related functions.
  • To assist us in analyzing how our Service is used.

We want to inform users of this Service that these third parties may have access to their personal information, but solely to perform tasks assigned to them on our behalf. They are obligated not to disclose or use the information for any other purpose.

Security

The UADD implements reasonable security practices and measures to safeguard information provided through this mobile application against loss, misuse, unauthorized access or disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Users are responsible for maintaining the secrecy and security of their login credentials. If users disclose their credentials to third parties, the Department shall not be responsible for any loss, damages (including, but not limited to, loss of business projects, loss of profits, or any other damages) or consequences arising from the use of, or inability to use, the mobile application or any of its contents.

Policy Changes

UADD reserves the right to change this policy at any time by notifying users about the availability of a new privacy statement. Visitors are encouraged to check this site periodically to stay informed about the current privacy and security guidelines. This statement and related policies do not create any contractual or legal rights in favor of any party.

User Responsibility

You must:

  • Be a natural person to access or seek to access the MP Nagar Palika mobile app.
  • Not access or attempt to access (either directly or indirectly) any other person's MP Nagar Palika mobile app account, unless duly authorized.
  • Not permit any other person to use your login credentials and must keep them confidential at all times, as you are responsible for any activity conducted using your credentials.
  • Immediately report to the Help Desk if you suspect that the security of your account has been compromised, e.g., your account credentials have been lost or stolen.
  • Ensure that your personal details are accurate and up to date.
  • Use this mobile app and your account only for lawful purposes and in a manner that does not infringe upon the rights of, or restrict or inhibit, the use of this mobile app by any third party. This includes avoiding conduct that is unlawful, harassing, distressing, or offensive, including the transmission of obscene content or disruption of services.
